Our Journey

2015 - The Beginning

Founded by David Murungi with a small dinner in downtown, serving local dishes.

2018 - First Expansion

Moved to our current location, expanding our menu to include Mordern dishes like Apple Millet Porridge.

2019 - Award Recognition

Received the "Best Fine Dining Experience" award from The foodies Magazine for two consecutive years.

2021 - Pandemic Adaptation

Successfully pivoted to offer best outdoor dining, maintaining our quality.

2023 - Present Day

Continuing our tradition of excellence with a renewed focus on sustainable and locally-sourced ingredients.

Our Philosophy

At Dulkin, we believe that great food starts with respect - for ingredients, for tradition, and for innovation. Our culinary philosophy is built on three pillars:

  • Quality: We source only the finest ingredients from trusted local producers.
  • Creativity: Our chefs constantly experiment while respecting culinary traditions.
  • Sustainability: We're committed to reducing our environmental impact through responsible practices.
